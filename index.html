The Approach

Philosophy of Frame

01

Subtractive Lighting

True cinematic depth isn't about how much light you add, but how much you take away. I prioritize negative fill to create shapes that suggest mystery, allowing the audience's imagination to complete the frame.

02

Technical Integrity

Understanding the science of sensors, the breathing of glass, and the math of color science is the foundation that allows for artistic freedom. I believe in mastering the rules only to break them with intent.

03

Emotional Tempo

Movement must be motivated. Whether it's a static master or a frantic handheld sequence, the camera's kinetic energy must match the character's internal pulse.
